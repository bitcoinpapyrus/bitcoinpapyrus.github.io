I am a graduate student who created this website as a fun side project to unify the whole world with stories and blockchain technology. I want to show how blockchain is more than just a currency. It is a shared written record. I decided to use bitcoin because it is the most popular cryptocurrency. If you do not have any, you can get some at coinbase.

Ancient Romans, Egyptians, and Greeks tried to preserve writings on various materials. These include the famous Roman lead curse tablets that archeologists can uncover today. I think we should all write things we want into the blockchain, which ensures digital preservation and accessibility of our writings. Let the experiment begin.
